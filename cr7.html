Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time.
Born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, he has played for top clubs including Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and Al Nassr.
Known for his incredible speed, powerful shots, and goal-scoring ability, Ronaldo has won numerous titles, including multiple UEFA Champions League trophies and Ballon d'Or awards.
He is also the all-time leading goal scorer in international football and has represented Portugal in several major tournaments, winning the UEFA Euro 2016 and the UEFA Nations League in 2019.
